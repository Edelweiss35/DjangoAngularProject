Refund & Cancellation Policy

AudienceSutra.com, the parent company Audience Insight Technologies (OPC) Pvt Ltd (referred to here as AIT), will be able to consider refund/cancellation requests only within 30 days of payment for services to AudienceSutra.com. Once this timeline is past since the payment for the service by the client, unfortunately AudienceSutra.com or AIT will not be able to offer a refund.

The following criteria must be fulfilled for enabling the refund request of any payment already processed by AudienceSutra.com:

  • Payment is made in excess of the estimates and is mutually agreed by both AudienceSutra.com or AIT and the client for the amount that is in excess of the actual payment of the project.
  • AudienceSutra.com and AIT acknowledges that the actual deliverables of the project undertaken for the client is not met as per promise and therefore agrees to make the payment back to the client for the amount that is the shortfall, calculated pro rata and agreed by all parties including AudienceSutra.com and AIT.
  • AudienceSutra.com and AIT acknowledges that the project requested by the client is not possible to be undertaken at this point by AudienceSutra.com. Hence, on mutual agreement AudienceSutra.com will pay the money back to the client.

No other situation for request of refund will be entertained by the company. For any situation pertaining to dissatisfaction with the services, we have provided option of sharing feedback with us on feedback@audiencesutra.com.

In terms of cancellation, since this is an online service, cancellation is not allowed in normal circumstances once the project is initiated. Only in extraordinary circumstances, only if it is deemed so by AudienceSutra.com or AIT, it will be considered for cancellation. Even in such a situation, AudienceSutra.com or AIT reserve the right to deduct expenses so incurred for the time or resources AudienceSutra.com or AIT had to deploy until the point of cancellation request.

All refunds and cancellation processing require a time of 15 working days from AudienceSutra.com or AIT from the date of request, if accepted as is by AudienceSutra.com or AIT, or from the date of written agreement email from AudienceSutra.com or AIT. The transfers will be carried out through online NEFT banking protocol from the corporate account of AIT to the banking details provided through registered email of the client.
