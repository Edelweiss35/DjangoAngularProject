Mission

We want to empower the product & brand owners with real-time consumer insights for decision making in an environment where brands are being discussed, evaluated and recommended through online/social media platforms on a always on basis.

In today's data-driven world with businesses constantly on action mode, one tends to focus more on "What happened?" rather having the patience to understand the real cause of "Why?" an event happened.

  • 1. We help you discover the "Why?" before your precious resources are diminished and while it still matters.
  • 2. We share it in a informative, user friendly and dynamic dashboard.
  • 3. It is a cost effective tool with significant ROI given the nature of operation is primarly online.

This results in:

  • Short term fixes
  • Disenchanted customers
  • Inability to build loyalty
  • Costlier investment to fix it later
  • Concerned investors losing confidence

inspiration

Your customer’s behaviour is driven by your ‘Why’ and not by the ‘What?’ that we usually support him with.

We know for a fact that behaviour like trust and loyalty is driven by the lymbic part of the brain. It is the central part quite like the diagram with the ‘why?’ and ‘how?’. This part of the brain also drives all our decisions and actions.

The reason for our belief in iconic brands to buy any product/ service from them is because we believe in what they believe in.

The neo cortex or the ’what’ part of the diagram, is the rational and analytical part of the brain and also understands complex language.

Although customers perfectly understand all the technical details and rational explanations from most of the brands/products around us, we still aspire for only one brand which we are loyal to and we believe is for us.

This is the Inside Out approach, where the reason to be part of a brand/product is driven by the ‘why?’ and is then rationalised with ‘what?’ based on trust and loyalty.
